Samosas

Prepare within

A samosa sambusa, or samboksa is a fried or baked dish with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, lentils, macaroni, noodles, cheese, minced lamb or minced beef. Pine nuts can also be added. Its size and consistency may vary, but typically it is distinctly triangular or tetrahedral in shape.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flower
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Mix together the flour, oil and salt.
  2. Add a little water, until mixture becomes crumbly.
  3. Beat dough on a work surface and knead again.
  4. Add ginger, garlic, green chillies and few coriander seeds.
  5. Stir fry for 1 minute, add onions and saute till light brown.
  6. Add cilantro (fresh coriander), lemon juice, turmeric, red chili, salt and garam masala.
  7. Stir fry for 2 minutes. Add potatoes
  8. Deep fry the samosas on low to medium heat until light brown.
